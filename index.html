By Ruben Gonzalez


Overview

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive is a multiplayer first-person shooter developed by Valve that’s focused on strategic and competitive gameplay. As such, it quickly garnered a remarkable fan base and has become a staple for competitive video games.

Humble Beginnings

Counter-Strike began as a simple mod for Half-Life
...but in time it has grown to be so much more.

On it's way out?

Some players have begun doubting the longevity of CS:GO. On the contrary, it’s doing about as well as it ever has and even peaked at a record 1.3 million players this April.

Putting the Global in Global Offensive

CS:GO is played all around the world and is especially popular in Europe, but it also has rapidly growing playerbases in South America and Asia.

If you stream it, they shall come

There's a respectable audience for competitive CS:GO. Out of their respective major events, CS:GO was the 2nd most watched esport in 2019. Fans also streamed nearly 24 million hours of it through May.
